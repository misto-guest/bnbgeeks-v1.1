**Date:** February 2, 2026
**Purpose:** Analyze existing Dutch advertorial blogs to identify successful advertisers, content strategies, and pricing models

## Executive Summary

Analysis of 4 Dutch advertorial blogs reveals **5 key advertiser categories** driving the market:

1. **B2B Services** (construction, industrial, professional services)
2. **Financial Products** (investing, banking, real estate funds)
3. **E-commerce & Retail** (home improvement, lifestyle, children's products)
4. **Technology & SaaS** (software, marketing tools, digital services)
5. **Local Businesses** (regional exposure, services)

**Key Finding:** The most profitable niches align with **high-ticket B2B services** and **financial products**, confirming our FinTech and SaaS recommendations.
