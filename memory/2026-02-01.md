## Market Research: Dubai Off-Plan Demand

### Top Demand Locations (2024-2025)
1. **Jumeirah Village Circle (JVC)** - High investor demand, affordable entry
2. **Business Bay** - Premium location, 44% premium prices
3. **Dubai Hills Estate** - Family-friendly, strong appreciation
4. **Damac Hills 2** - Suburban villas, gaining traction
5. **Downtown Dubai** - Luxury segment, high capital appreciation

### International Buyer Demographics
- **UK buyers** - Seeking tax efficiency + ROI
- **Indian investors** - Largest buyer group, proximity
- **Russian buyers** - High-net-worth, seeking safe haven
- **Chinese investors** - Growing interest, lifestyle + investment
- **Pakistani buyers** - Strong cultural ties, end-users + investors

### Market Stats (2024-2025)
- Off-plan = 64-69% of total residential sales
- 226,000 total sales in 2024 (+36% YoY)
- Q2 2024: 25,466 off-plan deals worth AED 52B (+81% YoY)
- 2025 Q1: 69% of transactions were off-plan
- Luxury segment (>AED 10M): +31% YoY growth

### Key Selling Points for Course
- Payment plans (60/40, 50/50, post-handover)
- Capital appreciation potential (34%+ for apartments)
- Rental yields (6-10% net)
- Tax efficiency (0% property tax, 0% capital gains)
- Golden visa eligibility

### Risks to Cover (Unbiased)
- Oversupply risk in peripheral areas
- Project delays
- "Off-plan fatigue" - slowing growth in some areas
- Price corrections possible in 2026

### Top Countries for Marketing
1. **United Kingdom** - High interest, professional investors
2. **India** - Largest buyer base, cultural familiarity
3. **Russia** - High-net-worth, seeking diversification
4. **China** - Emerging market, luxury buyers
5. **Pakistan** - Strong end-user demand
