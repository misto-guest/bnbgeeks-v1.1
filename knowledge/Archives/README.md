# Archives

*Completed projects and inactive areas*

## What goes here
- Completed projects (move from Projects/)
- Inactive areas (move from Areas/)
- Old resources that are no longer relevant

## Structure
Keep original structure with date suffix:
```
Archives/
├── 2025-12-project-name/
├── 2026-01-area-name/
└── legacy/
```

## When to archive
- **Projects**: When completed or cancelled
- **Areas**: When no longer relevant (e.g., moved to a new job)
- **Resources**: When outdated or superseded

## Retention policy
- Keep for at least 1 year
- Can delete after 2+ years if truly obsolete
- When in doubt, keep it
